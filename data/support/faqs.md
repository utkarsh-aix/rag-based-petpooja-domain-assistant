# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## What type of restaurants can use Petpooja?
Petpooja is suitable for restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, food courts, and quick service restaurants.

## Is Petpooja a cloud-based platform?
Yes, Petpooja is a cloud-based restaurant management system accessible across devices.

## Does Petpooja support multi-outlet restaurants?
Yes, Petpooja supports both single-outlet and multi-outlet restaurant operations.

## Can Petpooja handle online orders?
Yes, Petpooja provides centralized online order management and menu control.

## Does Petpooja provide inventory management?
Yes, Petpooja offers automated inventory tracking, low-stock alerts, and inventory reports.

## Is payroll management included?
Petpooja provides a dedicated payroll solution with attendance, leave, and shift management features.

## Does Petpooja support GST compliance?
Yes, Petpooja supports automated GST invoicing and reporting.
