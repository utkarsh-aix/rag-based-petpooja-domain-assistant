# Privacy Policy Summary

Petpooja, operated by Prayosha Food Services Private Limited, is committed to protecting user data across its platforms, including web, desktop, and mobile applications.

## Data Collection
Petpooja collects only necessary personal and business information required to provide its services.

Collected data may include:
- Name and company name
- Contact details (email, phone, address)
- Billing and payment information
- Usage and device information
- App activity and analytics data

## Use of Personal Information
Personal information is used to:
- Deliver and improve services
- Provide customer support
- Perform analytics and reporting
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Security
Petpooja uses industry-standard security practices, including secure servers, firewalls, and access controls. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention
- Personal data is retained only as long as necessary
- Identifiable data is deleted after service termination as per legal requirements
- Anonymous data may be retained for analytics

## User Rights
Users have rights including:
- Access to personal data
- Data correction
- Data deletion
- Restriction of processing
- Data portability
- Objection to processing

## Third-Party Services
Petpooja integrates with third-party service providers for payments, analytics, and support. Petpooja is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.
