# Product Overview

Product Name: Petpooja Restaurant Management System

Petpooja offers a suite of restaurant management products designed to automate daily restaurant operations and improve service quality.

---

## 1. Restaurant POS (Point of Sale)

Petpooja POS is a fast and easy-to-use restaurant billing software designed to handle high order volumes efficiently.

### Key Capabilities
- Quick billing with a 3-click workflow
- Order taking, bill generation, and KOT creation
- Table management (merge/split tables)
- Split and multiple payment options
- Discounts and coupon application

---

## Inventory Management
- Item-wise automatic stock deduction
- Low-stock alerts
- Day-end inventory reports
- Inventory consumption tracking

---

## Reports and Analytics
- Automated, paper-free reports
- Day-end sales reports
- Online order reports
- Staff activity tracking
- Inventory usage reports
- Access to 80+ business reports

---

## Online Ordering Management
- Centralized management of online orders
- Online menu control
- Order status tracking
- Payment collection and revenue monitoring

---

## App Marketplace and Add-ons
Petpooja provides additional modules to enhance restaurant operations, including CRM, analytics, and operational tools.

---

## 2. Payroll Management System

Petpooja offers a payroll and attendance management solution integrated with biometric devices.

### Core Features
- Biometric and location-based attendance
- Timesheets and attendance corrections
- Monthly and hourly payroll processing
- Overtime and late entry rules
- PF, ESIC, and PT salary breakdown
- Downloadable payslips

---

## Leave and Shift Management
- Paid and unpaid leave types
- Leave approval workflows
- Leave balance tracking
- Multiple shift creation and assignment
- Overnight and flexible shift support

---

## Employee Loans and Advances
- Track employee loans and advances
- Automatic salary deductions
- Transaction history logs

---

## Payroll Reports
- Employee-wise and department-wise reports
- Excel and Google Sheets export
- Customizable payroll reports

---

## Mobile Applications
### Owner App
- Monitor staff activity remotely
- View attendance and shift data
- Approve leave and attendance requests
- Track biometric device status

### Employee App
- Mobile punch-in and attendance
- Shift and leave visibility
- Leave request submission
- Location-based punch authorization
