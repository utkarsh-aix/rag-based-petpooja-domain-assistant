# About Petpooja

Company Name: Petpooja  
Legal Entity: Prayosha Food Services Private Limited  
Founded: 2011  
Founded In: Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India  
Founders: Parthiv Patel, Apurv Patel  
Industry: Restaurant Technology / SaaS  

Petpooja is a cloud-based restaurant management software company that helps restaurants improve operational efficiency and revenue growth.

## Company Background
Petpooja started in 2011 as a B2B food delivery platform handling bulk corporate food orders. During early operations, the founders observed that many restaurants struggled due to outdated billing systems and lack of operational technology.

Restaurants faced challenges such as:
- Bulky and outdated billing machines
- No clear visibility of profits and expenses
- Inefficient daily operations
- High business shutdown rates

To solve these problems, Petpooja built a user-friendly, cost-effective restaurant billing and management software designed specifically for small and medium-sized restaurants.

## Current Position
Petpooja has evolved into one of India’s leading restaurant technology providers, serving over 100,000 restaurants globally across various food service formats.

## Core Business
- Cloud-based POS and billing software
- Inventory and menu management
- Business analytics and reporting
- Operational automation for restaurants

## Mission
To provide technology that enables sustainability, growth, and operational efficiency in the food and beverage industry, especially for small and medium restaurants.

## Funding
Petpooja has received funding from investors including Udaan and Dharana.
