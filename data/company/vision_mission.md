# Vision and Mission

## Vision
To become the leading all-in-one restaurant management platform that empowers small and medium food businesses by simplifying operations and improving profitability through technology.

## Mission
Petpooja aims to:
- Simplify restaurant operations using a unified POS platform
- Reduce operational costs and errors
- Enable data-driven decision-making with real-time reports
- Support compliance such as GST invoicing
- Help restaurants manage billing, inventory, online orders, and CRM from a single screen

## Key Focus Areas
- Unified restaurant management platform
- Real-time analytics and owner dashboards
- Operational efficiency and automation
- Customer engagement and loyalty
- Accessibility across devices (PC, Android, iOS)
- Cloud-based scalability for SMEs
